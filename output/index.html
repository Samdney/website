Hi,

my name is Carolin and I'm an independent mathematical scientist (Research Scientist) working on designing a new kind of censorship free, access to knowledge warranty, privacy and anonymity preserving internet by a Mathematical Computer Science approach for Distributed Network Structures & Protocols as well as Algorithms, Combinatorics & Optimization.

From time to time, I also make art, mostly digital art about math cryptography, privacy and digital security, as well as the connection and integration of scientifc phenomena into artwork.
